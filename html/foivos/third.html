Internet of Things

IoT or Internet Of Things is about bringing the world into a new era of instant connectivity by connecting all devices to the Internet, unlocking new potential.
IoT aims at enabling the collection of data using sensors and remote control of devices.
With IoT you will be able to get notifications to your phone if someone tries to break into your house, check remotely if your fridge is empty or turn on the heater so that your home will be warm when you arrive.
